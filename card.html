Wolf

Wolfs can adapt very easily and quickly to weather, that's why they can live in a cold area as easy as the desert.

  • Scientific Name: Canis lupus
  • Avarage Lenght: 1,2-2 meters
  • Avarage Lifespan: 6-8 years
  • Habitat: Mountain areas, forests or deserts.

The wolf, also know as the grey wolf, is a larga canine native to Eurasia and North America. The males avering weight 40kg, females 37kg. It is carnivorous, so it uses to hunt his food. For security, they're usually in groups.
